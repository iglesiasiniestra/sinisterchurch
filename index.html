Who We Are

Welcome to the website of the Sinister Church. Our mother church is located in a protected area of the mountains of Puerto Rico. We have the best weather and the best view of the stars!

"Sinister" can represent something outside the norm or breaking from tradition. This can be seen as a strength for those who reject conventional paths in favor of truth or deeper understanding. Despite our unconventional name, the Sinister Church is not about causing harm or promoting negative behavior. Instead, we are a community of truth-seekers. We help our members discover the light within them by embracing the shadows around us and offer a welcoming space for those who feel disconnected from traditional churches. After all, darkness is the absense of light, but shadows are created by a shining light. The fear of embrindacing the shadows fades away when the final destination is to discover and learn to control our internal shining light.

We invite you to explore our website, learn about us and contact us with any questions you may have

~Pastor Siniestro~
